Important Information

Before you begin your application, please be aware of the following important points:

  • IATE is not a HEFCE registered institution. This means that, sadly, students aren't able to access government funding sources such as Student Loans to fund their course.
  • IATE is not a licensed student visa organisation. Unfortunately, this means that students cannot use our courses to apply for a visa to live and study in the UK.
