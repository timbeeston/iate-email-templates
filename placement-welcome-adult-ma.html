First-Year Placement Details

As a first-year trainee, you will be assigned a 6-month placement running from January to July.

This typically involves:

  • Working in a community-based service
  • Seeing two individual clients per week
  • Receiving fortnightly supervision with an IATE approved supervisor (registered with UKCP or HCPC for Art Therapists)
  • Maintaining a supervision ratio of 1 supervision hour to 4 client contact hours
